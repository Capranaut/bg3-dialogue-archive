    This is a fallback one-liner. In case the Player manages to approach her an initiate dialog during the goblin hunt celebration, Korrilla gives a cryptic one-liner to the player. No Hasmet node is needed, as she teleports away right after.

    How to trigger:

    Open the campnight debug book and cue up the raider version of the goblinhunt celebration campnight.
    Initiate the long rest, and watch the entry cinematic.
    Open ReCon and use "statusapply INVISIBILITY". Wait for Korrilla to teleport in at -637; 0; -162. Approach her while invisible, and initiate dialog with her.

    Go to -111; 24; -29 in SCL_Main_A which teleports Korrilla to-108; 24; -19. Approach Korrilla and initiate dialog. If you are not invisible, she should teleport away if she sees you approach.
