    This AD plays during the Ketheric Showdown in the lower Colony, when Ketheric uses his "Command Troops" ability to make his minions focus their efforts on attacking a specific target. This will only happen during the first phase, in Ketheric's human form.

    How to trigger:

    This AD plays during the Ketheric Showdown in the lower Colony, when Ketheric uses his "Command Troops" ability to make his minions focus their efforts on attacking a specific target. This will only happen during the first phase, in Ketheric's human form.

    - Load SCL_Main_A
    - Using ReCon command "oe KethericShowdown_Start" to teleport yourself there.
    - Go through the dialog, approach Ketheric, go through that dialog too. Combat will start.
    - It is advisable to enter godmode with ReCon command "god". - Wait in combat for Ketheric to use this ability.
