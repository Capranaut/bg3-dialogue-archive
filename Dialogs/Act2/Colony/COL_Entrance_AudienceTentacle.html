    • Synopsis:

    An interaction with a huge tentacle that is psychically connected to the Elder Brain. Will only appear at the Colony's Moonrise Assault entrance if we came from the Necro-Lab - the tentacle won't have broken through Moonrise Tower. The tentacle will disappear again once the Elder Brain departs.
    A psionic pulse will emanate from the tentacle when we approach it, and we will get psychically told some personal history from the Elder Brain.

    How to trigger:

    - Load up GustavDev/SCL_Main_A
    - Either use "oe Necromancer_Audience" in ReCon or go to the Audience route through the debugbook.
    - Escape or kill the cage you get teleported into.
    - Kill combat.
    - Go into the central corridor and to the Assault entrance of the Colony
    - Interact with S_COL_Entrance_Audience_Tentacle_c7291e48-45e1-43c5-b243-8b4716e1344e
