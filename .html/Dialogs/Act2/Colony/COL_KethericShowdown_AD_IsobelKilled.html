    • Synopsis:

    This AD plays during the Ketheric Showdown in the lower Colony, when the player kills Isobel during the combat. Ketheric will react to this in human form.

    How to trigger:

    This AD plays during the Ketheric Showdown in the lower Colony. It will play on Ketheric if Isobel is killed during the combat.

    - Load SCL_Main_A
    - Using ReCon command "oe KethericShowdown_Start" to teleport yourself there.
    - Use command "oe KethericShowdown_AudienceRoute" to make sure Isobel is here, and tadpoled.
    - Go through the dialog, approach Ketheric, go through that dialog too. Combat will start.
    - To check the Apostle form version, use "oe KethericShowdown_Transform".
    - Kill Isobel (either through ReCon or in-game combat). This AD will play on Ketheric.
