    • Synopsis:

    This AD plays during the Ketheric Showdown in the lower Colony, after Ketheric has transformed into his Apostle form/reached his second stage, Nightsong will react to this upon her first combat turn after this.

    How to trigger:

    This AD plays during the Ketheric Showdown in the lower Colony. It will play on Nightsong.

    - Load SCL_Main_A
    - Using ReCon command "oe KethericShowdown_Start" to teleport yourself there.
    - Use "oe KethericShowdown_Transform". This will initiate stage 2 and transform Ketheric into the Apostle of Myrkul.
    - Once Nightsong gets her first turn in combat (and she is available to speak), this AD will play on her.
