    Synopsis:

    The player encounters Shadowheart sheltering in the Druid Grove. When we find her, she seems greatly preoccupied with a strange githyanki artefact. She may join our party after we speak to her, but needs more convincing in the case that gith are present. Note: Much of this scene plays out in a similar manner to Shadowheart's previous recruitment opportunity closer to the site of the nautiloid crash - we can find her at the druid grove if we advance forwards without recruiting her - she makes her own way to the grove off screen. We may have met her previously, in which case she's suspicious as to why we keep bumping into one another. We may even have been forced to abandon her during our earlier escape from the ship - something she'll recall bitterly. Shadowheart is particularly reluctant to deal with gith players - they were her enemies during her prior secret mission to steal the strange artefact, though the player will not know this yet
