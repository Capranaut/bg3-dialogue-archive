    • Synopsis:

    After being freed from the Absolute's thrall, Minsc tears his lair apart in the search for Boo. He eventually leads us to a small chamber that had been bricked up, and cautiously creeps in - warning us that it is the lair of a dangerous beast (Boo.)

    How to trigger:

    Debug:
    1. CTRL+Shift+7
    2. Minsc->Recruit->Minsc & Boo Reunion
    3. Talk to Minsc

    Legitimate:
    1. Encounter Minsc in the Counting House and let him escape
    2. Encounter Minsc again in the Abandoned Cistern. Start combat with him and knock him out
    3. Start dialog with Minsc after knocking him out
    4. After the dialog help Minsc destroy the cracked walls in the Abandoned Cistern to find Boo
    5. Talk to Minsc
