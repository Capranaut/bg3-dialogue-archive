    • Synopsis:

    Minthara at party organized by Withers. This is the very last time we see Minthara in the game. She's in a reflective mood, and is warmer to many of the companions than previously. This is because of the bond she feels with them, having gone through a great crisis together, but also because she is now an exile and knows she needs to keep what few friends and allies she has close to her if she is to have any chance of surviving.

    She still has a sharp tongue, and won't take kindly to anyone acting superior to her - and she's still plotting to make herself more powerful, and willing to step on anyone who gets in the way. But she is very aware that her survival will be difficult in this new world, and she's intelligent enough not to antagonise the people who have most reason to trust her.
