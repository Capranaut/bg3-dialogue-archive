    In the stormshore tabernacle, as part of his behaviour, the temple guardian will pray. The guardian has no god at the moment, but we can make him to pray to "all gods" or to the god that fits more with the situation.

    The praying point is in front of the generic shrine, but can be moved to the other ones without problem.
